The IT Compass Test is inspired by the now famous Political Compass test, a great source of criticisms and memes. You will simply have to indicate (freely and according to your ideas) how much you agree with 20 propositions about the worlds of information technology, software, networks, internet, social networks, and the latest technologies. There are not right or wrong answers. At the end of the test, you will find the result based on your answers and some guidelines to understand it.
I made this website during my free time as a simple educational project. The source code is available on my GitHub profile. This test is not based on any scientific study and the results do not strictly represent the reality. Please, do not use this website as a penetration testing target: you will just waste my time and will not find anything interesting.
